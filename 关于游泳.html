游泳最先出现在什么时候？是什么时候成为一项体育赛事的呢？相信下面的内容会给你答案~ 项目历史： 古代游泳，根据现有史料的考证，国内外较一致的看法是产生于居住在江、河、湖、海一带的古代人。他们为了生存，必然要在水中捕捉水鸟和鱼类作食物，通过观察和模仿鱼类、青蛙等动物在水中游动的动作，逐渐学会了游泳。 中国历史悠久，水域辽阔。记载中游泳，始于五千年前。但游泳作为一个体育项目得以发展还是近一两百年的事。 17世纪60年代，英国不少地区的游泳活动就开展得相当活跃。1828年，英国在利物浦乔治码头修造了第一个室内游泳池，这种泳池到19世纪30年代，在英国各大城市相继出现。1837年，在英国伦敦成立了第一个游泳组织，同时举办了英国最早的游泳比赛。1896年，游泳被列为奥运会竞赛项目。 游泳的最高组织机构是国际游泳联合会，总部设在瑞士洛桑。1908年由比利时、丹麦、芬兰、法国、德国、英国、匈牙利和瑞典游泳协会倡议成立。 When did swimming first appear? When did it become a sporting event? I believe the following content will give you the answer ~ Project History: Ancient swimming, according to the existing historical data research, the domestic and foreign consensus is that the ancient people living in the river, river, lake, sea area. In order to survive, they must catch water birds and fish for food in the water. By observing and imitating the movement of fish, frogs and other animals in the water, they gradually learned to swim. China has a long history and vast waters. Recorded swimming began five thousand years ago. But it was only about a hundred or two hundred years before swimming developed as a sport. In the 1660s, swimming was very active in many parts of England. The first indoor swimming pool was built in Liverpool's George Pier in 1828, and by the 1830s it was appearing in major English cities. In 1837, the first swimming organization was formed in London, England, and the earliest swimming competitions were held in Britain. Swimming was added to the Olympic Games in 1896. The highest organization for swimming is the International Swimming Federation, based in Lausanne, Switzerland. It was founded in 1908 on the initiative of the swimming associations of Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Great Britain, Hungary and Sweden.
